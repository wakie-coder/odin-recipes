Guacamole

Ahhh guacamole, so simple yet so satisfying everytime you make it

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Onion and Roma Tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices: Fresh cilantro, minced garlic and cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and the tomatoes to your liking.
  2. Squash two or three avocados, depending on how much you would like to do.
  3. Add all the ingredients together.
  4. Done!
