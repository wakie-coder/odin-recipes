Bread

Making bread is easy, but is hard work

Ingredients

  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • A spoon of oil, whatever you like
  • Bread flour

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast by putting it in the warm water with a little bit of sugar, usually the plastic container give a specified amount to it so use that amount for both water and sugar, if not, for 500g of bread powder, you should use a teaspoon of sugar and a cup and a half of water and leave it to rest aproximately 10 to 15 minutes in ambient temperature.
  2. Make the bread dough by adding 500g of bread powder, two tablespoons of salt, the yeast with the water, and a teaspoon oil. Whisk all the ingredients until they form a dough-like texture.
  3. Once the dough is cohesive, use you countertop to keand the dough until the dough is soft and elastic.
  4. Let the though rise by putting it back in a container without a lid, instead use a wet towel and leave it in a warm enviroment for about an hour or an hour and a half.
  5. Punch the dough, and leave it back on the container that you would like to use for baking, same time.
  6. Bake the bread in the container with 375 degrees for about 30 to 35 minutes.
  7. Done
